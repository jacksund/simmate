Data Access

All data is accessible for free.

Many datasets are made available by third-parties — such as the Materials Project, OQMD, JARVIS, and more. We will never charge for the access of data that is already free & open. Be sure to thank & properly attribute the original communities!

Importantly, some datasets are only available in the Self-Hosted version of Simmate — such as AFLOW, eMolecules, Enamine, and more. This is because some sources do not allow redistribution of their data. While we cannot display this data on our public website, the Simmate Python client still lets you download datasets directly from third-party providers for your private use.
